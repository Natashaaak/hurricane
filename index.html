Hurricane Isabel Visualization

This application visualizes Hurricane Isabel, which struck in September 2003.

The scene focuses on the Southeastern United States, displaying:

  • Wind direction and strength as streamlines
  • Temperature as a cut plane

Data Source

Weather Research and Forecasting (WRF) model data

  • Coverage: 2000 × 2000 × 19.8 km
  • Resolution: 250 × 250 × 100 grid points

Camera Controls

  • Left click + drag: Rotate camera
  • Right click + drag: Pan camera
  • Mouse wheel: Zoom in/out
  • Touch (1 finger): Rotate
  • Touch (2 fingers): Pan & zoom

Visualization Controls

Use the control panel on the right to adjust:

  • Streamline density and appearance
  • Temperature cut plane position
  • Contour line visibility
