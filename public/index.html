This application visualizes Hurricane Isabel, which struck in September 2003.
The scene focuses on the Southeastern United States, displaying:
  • Wind direction and strength as streamlines
  • Temperature as a cut plane
The data is sourced from the Weather Research and Forecasting (WRF) model, covering an area of
2000 × 2000 × 19.8 km.
Camera controls:
  • Left button: Pan the camera
  • Middle button: Zoom
  • Right button: Move the camera
Users can adjust various visualization parameters, including the position and contouring of the temperature cut plane, as well as streamline settings such as density, radius, and other properties.
